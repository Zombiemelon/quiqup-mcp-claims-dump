<objective>
Ship the reference-data substrate for Phase 1: the 8 ADDR tools (account addresses CRUD-lite, country/state/city geo-lookups, and the Google Places place-detail lookup) plus the 5 ORDL reason-code lookups that downstream write phases need to validate enum inputs.

Purpose: The Quiqdash address book and every order's geographic context come from these endpoints. Phase 4 (write-path orders) cannot validate `service_kind`, `cancellation_reason`, `on_hold_reason`, `return_to_origin_reason`, `courier_failure_reason`, or country/state/city names without these lookups existing first.

Output: 13 new `lib/tools/*.ts` files, 1 new `lib/clients/google-places.ts` (the one-off non-Quiqup-auth client), two Vitest suites (one for the 12 platform-auth tools, one for the Google Places isolation), updated `app/[transport]/route.ts` and `evals/snapshots/tool-surface.json`, and a new `GOOGLE_PLACES_API_KEY` row in `.env.example`.
</objective>
